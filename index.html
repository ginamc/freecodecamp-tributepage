Dana Scruggs
Brooklyn-based photographer

Screenshot of Dana Scrugg's website, www.danascruggs.com.

Dana Scruggs is a brooklyn-based Photographer and first black photographer to shoot the cover of Rolling Stone. In 2016, Scruggs launched a magazine, SCRUGGS Magazine, that celebrates her vision of the male form.

According to her About Me page, she has also photographed for ESPN, New York Times, GQ, and more.

Dana was the first black female photographer to shoot for ESPN's 10th editition of the Body Issue. She photographed the fastest woman in the world, Olympic gold-medalist Tori Bowie.
